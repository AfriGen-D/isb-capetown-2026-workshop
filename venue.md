## About the Hotel

The 19th Annual International Biocuration Conference is hosted at the
**Lagoon Beach Hotel Conference Centre and Spa**, a beachfront venue in
Milnerton offering panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean, Table Mountain,
and the Cape Town skyline.

Located just **~20 minutes from Cape Town International Airport**, with easy
access to the city centre, the hotel combines professional conference
facilities with modern, comfortable accommodation, restaurants, a spa, and
recreational activities -- an ideal setting for delegates.

## This Workshop

The **AfriGen-D tutorial** takes place in the **Atlantic I** room within
the hotel's conference centre. Room signage will direct participants from
the main hotel lobby.

## Getting There

- **From Cape Town International Airport (CPT):** ~20 minutes by car.
  Taxis, Uber, and pre-booked airport transfers are all available.
- **From central Cape Town:** ~15--20 minutes by car.
- **Parking:** on-site at the hotel.

## Local Host

Although the venue is the Lagoon Beach Hotel, the local academic host is the
**African Bioinformatics Institute (ABI)** at the University of Cape Town.
The workshop is organised by **Mr Mamana Mbiyavanga** (UCT / AfriGen-D).

## While You're in Cape Town

Milnerton sits on the shore of Table Bay, with the V&A Waterfront, Table
Mountain, and Cape Town's city bowl all within a short drive. If your
schedule permits, consider extending your stay -- the conference dates fall
in South Africa's autumn (pleasant daytime temperatures, cooler evenings).
